In today's digital age, the rapid growth of technology has led to an increased reliance on social media for communication. However, this can come at the cost of face-to-face interaction, ultimately affecting productivity. According to recent reports, distractions caused by social media can decrease productivity. To mitigate this, effective time management is crucial for employees. Fortunately, there are several strategies to enhance communication skills, such as maintaining a daily journal to articulate thoughts and attending workshops to learn new techniques. By implementing these small adjustments, individuals can achieve better outcomes and a more fulfilling life.
